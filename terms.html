Terms of Service

Last updated: 2026-07-18

This application ("the App") is a personal video-editing tool built and operated by an individual creator to repurpose long-form podcast content into short-form video clips for the "Bendito Fantasy" (FPL en Español) YouTube and TikTok channels.

What the App does

The App processes video files supplied by its operator, generates short clips with captions, and — with explicit manual action by the operator — publishes those clips to the operator's own YouTube and TikTok accounts using the respective platform APIs.

Single-user tool

This App is not a public service. It is used solely by its operator to manage their own content and their own connected accounts. It does not accept signups, does not serve third-party users, and does not publish on behalf of anyone other than its operator.

Data use

The App only accesses the YouTube and TikTok accounts explicitly authorized by its operator via each platform's OAuth flow, and only to upload video content the operator has created. No data is sold, shared, or used for any purpose other than operating this tool.

Contact

Questions about this tool can be directed to the repository owner via GitHub.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-18

This application ("the App") is a personal video-editing tool built and operated by an individual creator to repurpose long-form podcast content into short-form video clips for the "Bendito Fantasy" (FPL en Español) YouTube and TikTok channels.

Who this applies to

The App is not a public service. It has exactly one user: its operator. It does not collect data from, or about, any third party.

What data the App accesses

  • Video and transcript files the operator supplies directly, stored locally on the operator's own machine.
  • OAuth access/refresh tokens for the operator's own YouTube and TikTok accounts, obtained through each platform's official authorization flow and stored locally on the operator's machine. These tokens are used only to upload video content the operator has created.

What the App does not do

  • It does not collect data from end viewers of the published videos.
  • It does not sell, share, or transmit any data to third parties.
  • It does not use collected data for advertising or profiling.
  • It does not retain data beyond what is needed to operate the tool locally.

Data retention & deletion

All data (video files, transcripts, and authorization tokens) is stored locally by the operator and can be deleted at any time by removing the relevant files. Revoking the App's access via YouTube/TikTok account settings immediately invalidates its ability to publish further content.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to the repository owner via GitHub.
